WHY YOU SHOULD DOWNLOAD CORA

Once the lock-downs imposed by almost all governments are over,  you will become exposed again to more and more persons who may pose a risk to you.   CORA helps you to measure and display this risk to allow you to take appropriate measures to manage this risk.    To install CORA,  just click on the DOWNLOAD button and follow the instructions.

If you have a previous version of CORAVITA installed,  de-install this app from your mobile thru the SETTINGS menu, before you download.

If you think,  that CORA is of no use to you,  because you have one of the government sponsored apps installed already, please consider the following:

The way CORA collects information from around you is the same as in the government sponsored apps using Bluetooth every five minutes to identify people close to you.   The output of either app is also the same,  as both apps send you an alert message.

So, what is the difference ?.

CORA calculates your risk based on the number of contacts and the average infectiousness of the population around you.   The government apps store the identity of the mobile phones who have been deemed ‘close contacts’.    In order to alert you,  a person which is on the list of your ‘close contacts’ and which tested positive,  will have to enter this information into his/her mobile. If this is not entered,  you will never be alerted.   If a person who is not infected,  accidentally or by purpose,  enters into his/her mobile,  that s/he is positive,  you will get an alert,  which I am sure is not a happy moment,  given that ‘it may have been just meant as a joke’ by this person.

In essence,  to safeguard your health,  you are dependent on the reliability of persons you do not know.    In contrast,  with CORA,  no other person has to do anything to calculate and display your risk.    CORA only uses data not subject to manipulation by other people.

If this makes sense to you,  but you do not want to de-install the government sponsored app,  don’t worry.    You don’t have to.    CORA can be installed while you keep the other app on your mobile.
